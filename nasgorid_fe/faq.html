FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the available payment methods?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, e-wallets (GoPay, OVO), and cash on delivery.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email where you can track the status of your delivery.

What if I need to cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order by contacting our customer support within 30 minutes after placing the order.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian dishes available. Check out our menu section for details.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No, there is no minimum order amount. You can order as little or as much as you like.
